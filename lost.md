# Losing things

* Student ID
    * Go to the IC during business hours and pay 20$ for a new one.
* Michigan License
    * You can do this [online](https://onlineservices.michigan.gov/ExpressSOS/) since you have your license number memorized.
* California License
    * You have to go to the DMV. Congrats.
* Credit cards
    * > Now I have my online bills on one CC that stays home so that I don't have to update everything after that.
    * MSUFCU
        * Call them or msg them on the app. They're usually friendly.
    * Discover
        * In the app. Usually gets here in 2-3 business days.
    * Chase
        * Haven't lost this one yet.
* Library card
    * They know you. Have to go to the front desk.
* Health insurance card
    * Some clickable thing on the website. Pretty easy to get.
    * You also can print something out in the meantime.
